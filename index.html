True or False?
"AI models are trained on recognizing patterns and cannot expose the raw data they were trained on."
Answer: FALSE
Through model inversion attacks, adversaries can reconstruct sensitive training data — including private records — directly from a model's responses. What goes in does not stay hidden.

Section I · Takeaways
What This Means for You
🔑
Understand how your data is being used to train AI models
  • Check the terms & conditions of every AI tool you use — many train on your inputs by default
  • Enterprise licenses (e.g. ChatGPT Enterprise, Claude for Work, Microsoft 365 Copilot) typically opt you out of model training
  • When in doubt, treat the AI like a public forum — don't submit data you wouldn't share openly
